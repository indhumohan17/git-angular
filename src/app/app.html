What is green energy?
Green energy refers to energy sources that are naturally replenished and produce minimal environmental impact, such as solar, wind, hydro, and geothermal power.
How does green energy benefit the environment?
Green energy reduces greenhouse gas emissions, air pollution, and reliance on fossil fuels, contributing to a healthier planet and combating climate change.
